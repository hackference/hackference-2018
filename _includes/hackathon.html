Hackathon



The hackathon is one of the biggest public hackathons in the United Kingdom - you don't have to be a student, or even a coder to come along and get involved!

We will have plenty of API's and technology for you to put your skills to, for the chance to win some amazing prizes, or even just impress your peers!

We don't have any parking at Impact Hub, but Digbeth has plenty of Carparks or you can use the Selfridges carpark by the Bullring.
